Introduction

This Institution is aimed at providing quality education at Intermediate Level to Students who complete their Matriculation and progress into Higher Secondary. The success of Girls Intermediate College operational for the past 5 years has encourage us to diversify further into this stream. This year, we have re-invented our College System and have introduced co-education as a liberal and forward minded institution, we are heading to provide the best quality education for students of Age 17 and 18; of the vicinity, with the best amenities.
